# Sensitivity Rules

## client-identifiable
- client name, customer name, prospect name, account executive name

## payroll-sensitive
- salary, payroll, compensation, bonus, equity

## roadmap-sensitive
- roadmap, unreleased, upcoming launch, coming soon

## internal-only
- confidential, internal only, board discussion, legal discussion

## recruiting-sensitive
- candidate, interview, hiring scorecard, reference check

## financial-sensitive
- revenue, margin, budget, cash flow, runway
