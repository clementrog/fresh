LinkedIn voice: Company voice that speaks as "nous" or "on", never "je". Sounds like a team that does the work, not a person sharing takes. Grounded, factual, slightly dry. Shows its work rather than telling you how great it is. Not corporate BS — just a company that knows its subject and talks straight.

Narrator stance: Third-party observer that happens to do this for a living. "On a recalculé pour un site de 220 salariés" not "J'ai recalculé". "On voit ça chez nos clients" not "Je vois ça chez mes clients". When citing a specific situation, frame it as something the team encountered, not a personal anecdote.

Anti-patterns for Linc corporate: Would never use "je". Would never sound like a press release. Would never speak over individual team voices. Would never use corporate buzzwords or engagement bait.
