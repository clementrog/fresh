# Linc Editorial Doctrine

- Prefer concrete proof over generic advice.
- Keep French as the default drafting language.
- Prioritize recent customer, product, and market evidence.
- Avoid broad motivational content without a traceable internal source.
- Human selection in Notion remains authoritative after opportunity creation.
- Posts are conversations, not reports.
- One post = one idea = one feeling.
- If it reads like it could come from any company, rewrite it.
- Specificity beats comprehensiveness. A single real detail beats 10 bullet points.
- The reader's test: would you stop scrolling for this?
- LinkedIn is not a whitepaper channel. If you need sections, you need a blog post.
